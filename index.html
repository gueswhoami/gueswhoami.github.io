Cybersecurity Topics You Should Know

  • 1. Password Security: Use strong, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication to protect your accounts.
  • 2. Phishing Awareness: Be cautious of suspicious emails or links; always verify the sender before providing personal information.
  • 3. Malware Protection: Install reputable antivirus software and keep it updated to guard against viruses and ransomware.
  • 4. Secure Networks: Use encrypted Wi-Fi connections and avoid public networks when handling sensitive activities.
  • 5. Software Updates: Regularly update your operating system and applications to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • 6. Data Backup: Keep regular backups of important files to recover from data loss or cyberattacks.
  • 7. Social Engineering: Be wary of manipulation tactics used by attackers to gain your trust and sensitive information.
